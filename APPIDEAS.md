# App Ideas - Intro
A running incomplete list of apps I've had. Nothing necessarily spectacular but deciding now in July 2022 to list out some of my ideas of the last decade both for my own edification and to perhaps share them to co-create. Perhaps I begin and open-source them, sell/offer them, or just keep sitting on them.

My dream is to make my ideas real to benefit humanity and just to feel fulfilled doing something with the creative inspiration I've been gifted. Perhaps I pursue Ycombinator or other idea incubators (in hindsight I should've been doing this over the last decade), perhaps I can get hired by a company to provide me with necessary resources, or perhaps I win the lottery and can form my own teams. Is anyone out there? Help wanted.

## App Ideas
(will add descriptions and continually update list over time)

- CleanCity: A website & mobile app platform to incentivize people to clean up litter in a fun, gasified way with an energetically & monetarily sustainable model. Money isn't the only incentive, and I've thought of various ways to encourage this grass-roots effort. Not everyone in the world would participate but the target audience isn't everyone. The platform would include pseudo anonymous social elements with profiles and potentially teams/clans, a heat map of the cleanliness of street blocks which decays from green toward red over time, complex & robust artificial intelligences trained with machine learning for both (1) verifying the cleaning actions via user-submitted photos, satellite imagery and perhaps municipal-operated street cameras, and (2) detecting bad actors (through a number of behavioral analysis points - I can expand upon this later). Originally conceptualized in 2017, returning to this idea in 2022 desiring to perhaps integrate with or build along side the LitterCoin and OpenLitterMap projects running on the Cardano blockchain.
- FairCampaign: Politically-neutral platform to simply & intuitively display political platforms of candidates, on all levels local to national. I imagine it so clean and perfect in my head. Originally conceptualized in 2016.
- Canopy: Probably my favorite idea, still, 6 years later. Originally conceptualized in 2016, a social media platform that I believe is still very viable and needed in 2022. A combination of MySpace, Twitter and old Google Plus. A "canopy" of arrangeable and configurable panels, encouraging wild MySpace-like visual customization while still maintaining a semblance of platform cohesion. Not only are the panels of various app integrations or user-created blocks of content in various shapes, but the visibility/privacy of the panels and profile are richly and intuitively configured similar to Google Plus' vision utilizing Circles, or Facebook's later implementation of post privacy (and we are now seeing apps today attempt to utilize: Instagram and Snapchat's close friends, etc).
- Aeva: Hardly unique, but an idea nonetheless - a Jarvis-like AI personal assistant to manage my emails, calendars, meal ideas & preparation, traffic reporting & navigation, house & vehicle status reports & management, uniquely tailored psychology hacks for personal motivation and time & energy management, and so much more. I actually developed a bit of this bot in 2017 using DialogFlow and a number of other technologies but, like all my other ideas, I set it aside.
- unnamed "Link in Bio" app integration: A collection of badges for displaying one's volunteering efforts. Originally conceptualized as an incentivized mechanism for the CleanCity idea - users can show on their social media profiles things like "Picked up over 100 bags of trash with CleanCity", expandable to virtually any non-profit/volunteering organization. I'm likely going to develop this.
